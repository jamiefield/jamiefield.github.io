Biography

I am an Assistant Professor of Business Data Analytics in the Management Information Systems Department of the John Chambers College of Business and Economics at West Virignia University. My research interests include multivariate machine learning methods and algorithms. Specifically, I’m interested in developing new methods of clustering, classification, and regression that have applications in business operations and healthcare, and how these methods can be deployed and used in operational settings.

Before moving to West Virginia, I was a Clinical Assistant Professor and Director of the Master’s of Science in Business Analytics in the Department of Management Science at the University of Miami School of Business Administration. I recieved my P.hD. from the University of Minessota School of Statistics under the guidence and supervision of Adam J. Rothman and Charles J. Geyer. I am also an undergraduate alumni of WVU where I have dual degrees in Industrial Mathematics and Statistics and Mathematics.
